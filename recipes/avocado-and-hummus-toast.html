Avocado and hummus toast

description:

Start the day with this protein-packed avocado toast that's ready in five minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 (38g each) slices wholegrain sourdough bread, toasted
  • 11 ⁄2 tbs hummus
  • 1 ⁄4 avocado, sliced
  • Mixed salad leaves, to serve
  • 1 tsp hemp seeds
  • 1 lime wedge

Instructions:

  1. Spread the toast with hummus. Top with the avocado. Scatter over mixed leaves, hemp seeds. Squeeze over lime to serve.
