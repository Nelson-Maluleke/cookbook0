    <h1 class="title">Beef and cheddar patties</h1>
    <img class="pic" src="https://i.postimg.cc/kgkYTTjr/beef-and-cheddar-patties-132550-1.jpg"
    alt="picture of Beef and cheddar patties">

    <hr>
      <h3>description:</h3>
      <p>Cheesy meat patties that only require 20 minutes prep and 20 minutes cooking time.</p>
    <hr class="bottom">

    <hr>
      <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
      <ul class="unordered">
        <li class="ingredients">500g beef mince</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 small brown onion, grated</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1/3 cup grated cheddar</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 garlic clove, crushed</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 1/4 cups fresh breadcrumbs</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 tbsp smoky barbecue sauce</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce</li>
        <li class="ingredients">1 egg, lightly beaten</li>
        <li class="ingredients">2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil</li>
      </ul>
    <hr class="bottom">

    <hr>
    <h3>Instructions:</h3>
    <ol class="ordered">
      
      <li class="instructions">Place mince, onion, cheddar, parsley, garlic, breadcrumbs, sauces and egg in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Mix to combine. Shape mixture into 6 patties. Place on a plate. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.</li>

      <li class="instructions">Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook patties, in 2 batches, for 4 to 5 minutes each side or until browned and cooked through. Serve.</li>
    
    </ol>
